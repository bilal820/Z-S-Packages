Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our packaging solutions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies by product type. For standard products, MOQ is typically 500 units. For custom designs, we can work with quantities as low as 250 units.

How long does production take?

Production time depends on the complexity of your order. Standard products: 2-3 weeks. Custom designs: 3-5 weeks including prototyping and approval time.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our in-house design team can create custom packaging designs from scratch or work with your existing designs to optimize them for production.

What materials do you work with?

We work with various materials including corrugated cardboard, Kraft paper, paperboard, and recycled materials. We can help you select the best option for your needs.
