About Z & S Packages

Crafting packaging excellence since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by industry veterans Zachary Smith and Samuel Johnson, Z & S Packages began as a small workshop with a big vision: to revolutionize the packaging industry with innovative, reliable, and sustainable solutions.

What started as a two-person operation has grown into a leading packaging manufacturer serving businesses across North America and Europe. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our success.

Today, we operate from a state-of-the-art 50,000 sq. ft. facility equipped with the latest manufacturing technology, allowing us to produce high-quality packaging at scale while maintaining the attention to detail that set us apart from day one.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable, and reliable packaging solutions that protect products, enhance brands, and exceed customer expectations.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in custom packaging solutions, recognized for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer partnership.
